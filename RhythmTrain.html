RhythmTrain

Upcoming publication: Godfrey, R, Rimmer, M., Headleand, C. and Fox, C. RhythmTrain: making rhythmic sight reading training fun. Proceedings of the International Conference on Computer Music (ICMC) 2022. (In press.)

RhythmTrain is an open-source sight-reading trainer app designed to help users learn and improve their classic western music notation reading abilities. The game is built in Godot, with Supabase being used to store and retrieve user data. It has a robust set of features ranging from leaderboards, levelling, badges and dynamic difficulty adjustment as well as a variety of modes to help players improve their sight-reading.
