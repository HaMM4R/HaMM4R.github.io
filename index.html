About Me

Hi! I’m Reece, I’m a masters by research student at the University of Lincoln looking at gamifying musical sight-reading practice. Since tinkering with game engines all the way back in 2012, I’ve been hooked on building software and have experience working in teams, with clients and independently on various projects. Outside of building software, I spend my time playing drums (and dabble with guitar) in Lincoln’s worst named band, No Flamingos on a Sunday. We’ll play anything from Motorhead to Britney Spears, with some originals thrown in for good measure, though I like to think we sound better than the name does!

More information my masters research can be found in the RhythmTrain project and if you’d like to see it in action, checkout the trailer below!
